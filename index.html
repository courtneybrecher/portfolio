Cloudvirga dashboard design

Last summer, I interned as a UX designer at Cloudvirga, a fintech startup focused on simplifying the home loan and credit card application processes. One of my key projects involved collaborating with the design team to redesign Cloudvirga’s platform, aligning it with the company’s updated branding, values, and mission. A primary goal of the redesign was to encourage users to complete their home loan applications. I played an integral role in every stage of the design process, from participating in regular design reviews to revising Figma files to improve the user interface. I also ensured the site met ADA compliance standards, creating a more inclusive experience for all users.
